FACULTY CORNER

“To me robotics in this automation world means precise position control with good dynamics. Though there are an endless number of things to discover about the robotics. Being a control system expert I get fascinated with the way students design controllers for the robots. In the quest of many possibilities, and also looking into the enormous possibilities of innovation and technology, the achievements of our Robotics Club, since its foundation in 2016, have been exemplary. Throughout the course of these years, the club has managed to nurture curious minds allowing hundreds of projects to prosper. With these projects, students have not only gained and honed their skills in various domains of the field but also have managed to win accolades in multiple national level events. The multidisciplinary technological aspects attract students of almost all branch of engineering to nurture their skills. The previous accomplishment and the present endeavour of the club makes me feel nothing but proud, and for their future, I wish them best of luck"

Prof. Rajesh Gupta
President, Student Activity Centre(SAC)
Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering

“Where there's a will, there's a way! With strong will power the Robotics club MNNIT is preparing itself for the battles in the robotics world. Starting from the skeletal system to fabrication everything is done from scratch and with dedication. The honours students have received in various events is the proof of their level of excellence. It's the thought of empowering the lives of those who need that drives the students. No trouble can stop the club from achieving its goals. I feel honoured to be the Faculty Incharge of Technical Activities of students as a witness for commitment and potential of Robotics club. I wish them all the best for the upcoming challenges. May the force be with you!"

Dr. Venkateswara Rao Komma
Faculty Incharge Technical Activities, SAC
Associate Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering

“Technology like art is a soaring exercise of the human imagination.” Robotics Club MNNIT is a place where this art is imparted to the curious minds. The enthusiastic and talented students of this club implement their technical knowledge to solve the challenging problems of the real world. From humanoids to self-driving cars, they have displayed their ingenuity by creating a wide range of robots. The projects of the club have won several accolades at national level events. It gives me immense pride to be the faculty incharge of the club, where innovation is fostered and students strive persistently towards excellence."

Dr. Basant Kumar
Faculty Incharge, Robotics Club
