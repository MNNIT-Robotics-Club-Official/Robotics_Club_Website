FACULTY INCHARGE


  • FULL NAME
  • BRANCH

Area of Interest:

Power Electronics Converters, Control in Power Electronics, Systems and Control, Power Quality, Grid Interface, Solar and Wind Power Conversion, Virtual Instrumentation.

“To me robotics in this automation world means precise position control with good dynamics. Though there are an endless number of things to discover about the robotics. Being a control system expert I get fascinated with the way students design controllers for the robots. In the quest of many possibilities, and also looking into the enormous possibilities of innovation and technology, the achievements of our Robotics Club, since its foundation in 2016, have been exemplary. Throughout the course of these years, the club has managed to nurture curious minds allowing hundreds of projects to prosper. With these projects, students have not only gained and honed their skills in various domains of the field but also have managed to win accolades in multiple national level events. The multidisciplinary technological aspects attract students of almost all branches of engineering to nurture their skills. The previous accomplishment and the present endeavour of the club makes me feel nothing but proud, and for their future, I wish them best of luck."
