ABOUT US

Robotics Club MNNIT is a student body under the Student Activity Centre (SAC), MNNIT Allahabad. We are a group of like-minded engineering students from all the college departments, and what unites us is our passion for technology, particularly robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence. We build robots for all sorts of applications, not only for competing at competitions but also to solve challenging real-life problems with technology. Since our club's establishment in 2016, we have completed 50+ projects in all domains related to robotics and automation, from design to circuits to software. We are also the birthplace of a fully functional startup, TSAW.

Technologies like robotics, automation, IoT, and artificial intelligence will be the workhorses of this decade. Many challenges are waiting to be solved with technology. In the past four years, since our club's establishment, we have gained expertise in all aspects of technology related to robotics and automation, notably these.
