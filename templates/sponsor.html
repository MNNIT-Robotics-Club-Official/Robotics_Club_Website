Who are we?

Robotics Club, MNNIT is a student body under the Student Activity Centre (SAC), MNNIT Allahabad. The club serves as a cohort workspace for students whose ideas are not confined to their branch and are ready to genuinely do interdisciplinary work that’s impactful in all senses. We help students understand modern technologies, like robotics, artificial intelligence, automation, IoT, etc. (Link) through theoretical classes, discussions, and projects that transforms them into 21st century industry leaders.

Starting in 2016, our club has not only earned fame in many national level competitions (Link) and completed 50+ projects (Link), but most importantly, it has been a source of innovation on campus with state-of-the-art projects like autonomous vehicles, navigation robots, etc. We are proud to say that TSAW (Link), a fully functional startup, recognizes our club as its birthplace. Starting from a college club in 2016, we now want to expand way beyond it.

In the coming years we aim to:

1) Tie-up with industries and work on present challenges faced by industry in robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence.

2) Emerge as a center of excellence in the field of robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence, by doing cutting-edge research in our club, in both theoretical and practical domains.

3) Bring laurels to college and nation by participating not only in national but also international competitions.


How can You Help Us?

We ask for a donation suitable to your capability.

At Robotics Club MNNIT, we believe that donations are not just confined to finances.

Along with financial support, we shall be highly obliged for support in the technical domain such as, but not limited to :

1) Helping us purchase equipment for our projects

2) Any form of concession in equipments

3) Any form of technical mentorship

4) Helping us with industrial connections

5) Connecting us with other people who can help us

Why Sponsor Us?

“Any dream needs fuel to turn into fire.”

Throughout the past, the club and its activities have helped hundreds, if not thousands, of budding engineers, imparting onto them practical experience right from the day they enter college.

However, as we now want to expand beyond college premises and work on real-world problems, we need support because:

1) To work with industry we need industry-level equipment. which is costly.

2) Most robotics competitions need initial investment in the form of standard robots.

3) Being a student club, any industrial connections which can help us fetch industrial projects will help us a lot.

We are sure that we will not disappoint you, just like we never disappointed our past sponsors and esteemed alumni who had invested in us. Also, we assure you of our accountability towards anything you invest in us.

Robotics Club MNNIT has grown over the years and will continue to grow in the years to come with your support. If you choose to sponsor us, we'll promote you from all our social media accounts and our website's homepage too; this holds true regardless of whether you're an alumnus, individual or a company.
