ROBOMANIA

(AVISHKAR)

Avishkar is MNNIT's annual techno-management festival and is held either in September or October. Equipped with many technical and managerial events, Avishkar is an excellent platform for students to hone their skills, create outstanding projects, expand their networks, and build a superb CV.

All the college departments and technical clubs of MNNIT conduct multiple exciting competitions during Avishkar, which are open to students of MNNIT and students all across the nation. These competitions witness cut-throat competitors every year and truly bring alive the "Ideate, Innovate and Create" spirit amongst budding technocrats.

Apart from these competitions, many inspiring talks by eminent people in the industry and academia and other activities also happen during Avishkar, enlightening and preparing its participants for many challenges that lie ahead.

As a technical club, Robotics Club MNNIT organizes three challenging competitions during Avishkar under the name Robomania:

Autonomous Robotics Competition (Exclusively for 1st years)

This competition is exclusively for 1st year and serves as the first step for people stepping into the realm of robotics. The objective here is to build autonomous bots for relatively simple (but not-so-simple) scenarios like maze or wall follower robots.

Autonomous Robotics Competition (Open to All Years)

This competition is open to all years and involves advanced robotics concepts like machine learning, autonomous vehicles, planning, etc. The objective is to build an autonomous robot for some application which varies with every year's problem statement.

Autonomous-cum-Manual Robotics Competition (Open to All Years)

Ever thought about how the coolest-looking robots of Boston Dynamics dance? Or how do those industrial robotic arms execute complex maneuvers? Open for all years, this competition involves concepts like kinematics, robotic arm, either autonomous or manual.
